Reflection:

The way I made my wireframes is like top to the left side bottom, that is where your eyes will be first drawn, for example on my home page, you are first drawn to the navigation due to its strong colours, from there on, you are drawn by the text on the left side on the bottom then the picture on the right. The dark colour draws you in then the font comes. In the blogs section and the essay section, your eyes will move from top to bottom, because of how dark the navigation bar is, its strong therefore drawing you in, then the light text on the bottom slowly daws you in.
